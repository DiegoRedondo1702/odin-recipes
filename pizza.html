Pizza Recipes

Description

Making our own easy homemade pizza recipe is a much simpler process than we think, we just need to know the necessary pizza ingredients and the process to follow to integrate them. At RecetasGratis we want you to learn easy recipes that will allow you to prepare exquisite and unique dishes, such as different varieties of homemade pizzas. Therefore, here we show you how to make homemade pizza, are you going to miss it? Dishes prepared in an artisanal way always constitute a higher quality and more economical meal, so we encourage you to continue reading and discover the steps to make this homemade pizza in the oven with the pizza ingredients that you want. This way you will always know what to add to a homemade pizza!

Steps

  1. Grind the tomatoes in a food processor or blender. You can also mash them with a fork so that there are loose pieces. Add a pinch of salt and oregano to this sauce.
  2. Grate the mozzarella cheese.
  3. Chop all the other ingredients: the ham, onion and paprika into strips, and if you use pepperoni or sausage, into very thin slices, although they will probably have already sold it to you that way.
  4. Assembling the pizza
  5. Add the tomato sauce on your previously prepared pizza dough and ready in its respective mold. Spread the sauce well with the help of a spoon so that it is uniform on all sides.
  6. Next, add the grated mozzarella cheese and then the other ingredients.
  7. Place in the oven, previously preheated to 200 degrees Celsius.
  8. Let cook for 10-15 minutes. Make sure it doesn't burn, as some ovens are treacherous.
  9. The exact point of the pizza is: the cheese is melted and slightly golden on top, and the pizza dough is slightly brown on the bottom. It should be able to break easily without becoming a cookie.
